About

The overall idea and concept for the Steem Keychain browser extension was created by @yabapmatt who is a developer and witness on the Steem blockchain. It was primarily developed by @stoodkev who is also a Steem developer (SteemPlus) and witness. The design and development were funded by @aggroed (a top 20 Steem witness) and @yabapmatt through their joint business Steem Monsters, which is a blockchain-based collectible trading card game built on top of the Steem blockchain. Be sure to check it out at steemmonsters.com!
